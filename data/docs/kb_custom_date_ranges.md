# Feature Status: Custom Date Ranges in Reports

Reports currently supports only preset ranges: Last 7 days, Last 30 days, Last quarter.

Custom date range selection is on the public roadmap (Q3 2026) and is one of the top 5 most requested features from Team and Enterprise customers.

**Guidance for support/product ops:** Log these requests under the "custom-date-range" tag so Product can track demand. Do not promise a ship date. A temporary workaround is exporting weekly reports and combining them offline.

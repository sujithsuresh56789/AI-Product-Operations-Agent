# How to Invite Teammates to a Workspace

Workspace admins can invite teammates from **Settings > Members > Invite Member**. This requires the Team plan or higher.

Steps:
1. Go to Settings > Members.
2. Click "Invite Member" and enter their email address.
3. Choose a role: Admin, Editor, or Viewer.
4. The invitee receives an email with a link valid for 7 days.

If the Members tab is missing, the account may still be on the legacy Free/Starter plan; confirm the plan first before troubleshooting further.
